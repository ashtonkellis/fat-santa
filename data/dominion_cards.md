# Dominion — Complete Card List

Every card from the deck-building game Dominion (Donald X. Vaccarino, Rio Grande Games), grouped by expansion.

**Total cards in dataset:** 538 across 15 sets.

**Source:** Data scraped from the Dominion Strategy Wiki (wiki.dominionstrategy.com), packaged in the MIT-licensed PyPI 'dominion' project by Dan Foreman-Mackey (github.com/dfm/dominion).

**Snapshot note:** Covers expansions released through Menagerie (2020). Does NOT include Allies (2022), Plunder (2023), or Rising Sun (2023) — see the "Expansions not in this dataset" section at the bottom.

## Expansions not in this dataset

The dataset above is a 2020 snapshot (via the MIT-licensed `dominion` PyPI package, scraped from the Dominion Strategy Wiki). Dominion has since received three more large expansions. The current session's network policy blocked direct fetching of the wiki, so these are **not enumerated card-by-card** here to avoid recording unverified data. For completeness, they are:

| Expansion | Released | Approx. Kingdom cards | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Allies** | 2022 | 31 Kingdom-card piles + 23 Ally cards | Introduces Liaisons and Allies; several split piles (Augurs, Clashes, Forts, Odysseys, Townsfolk, Wizards). |
| **Plunder** | 2023 | 40 Kingdom cards | Adds Loot cards, plus new Events and Traits. |
| **Rising Sun** | 2023 | 25 Kingdom cards | Adds Prophecies and the Sun/shuffle mechanics, plus Events. |

To add these to the dataset, re-run in a session whose network policy allows fetching `wiki.dominionstrategy.com`, or drop in an updated `cards.json` from a newer build of the `dominion` package.
